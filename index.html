Research Information Sheet — Management Staff Survey

You are invited to participate in a PhD research study titled "Workplace Illness Trends and Productivity Impact in the Bangladesh RMG Sector — Management Perspective."


Purpose: This survey collects the observations and experience of line managers and supervisors regarding illness trends among workers in their line/section/department and the impact on absenteeism and production output.


Who should complete this: Line supervisors, line chiefs, section in-charges, floor managers, production officers, IE officers, and department heads who directly oversee workers.


Confidentiality: Your responses are completely confidential. No factory names, personal identities, or company-identifying information will be published. Data will be used for academic research only.


Voluntariness: Participation is entirely voluntary and will have no bearing on your employment.


Duration: Approximately 12–15 minutes.
